==================================================
RESEARCH OBJECTIVE
==================================================

Investigate how leading dropshipping / multichannel publishing software handle product images for marketplace listings, especially in channels like Mercado Libre, eBay, Amazon, Walmart, Etsy, Shopify marketplaces, and similar environments where image policy and conversion quality both matter.

The key question is:

How do the best systems choose, optimize, clean, rank, remediate, and publish images so that:
1. the listing complies with marketplace rules
2. the main image is attractive and conversion-friendly
3. the software can operate at scale without constant human correction

==================================================
RESEARCH QUESTIONS
==================================================

Investigate and answer all of these:

1. IMAGE SELECTION
How do strong platforms choose the primary image from a set of supplier/product images?
Do they rank images? By what criteria?
Do they detect text, logos, clutter, packaging, watermarks, hands, context backgrounds, or weak product framing?

2. MARKETPLACE POLICY COMPLIANCE
How do they adapt images to marketplace-specific rules?
Do they keep separate rules per marketplace?
Do they enforce different logic for main image vs secondary images?

3. REMEDIATION
When source images are imperfect, what do top systems do?
Do they:
- crop
- isolate product
- remove background
- remove text/logo
- replace background
- reject the item
- send to human review
- use AI editing
- use manual approval queues

4. ORDERING / SLOT ASSIGNMENT
How do they decide which image becomes cover/main image and which become secondary?
Do they swap image roles based on policy/commercial goals?

5. CONVERSION / ATTRACTIVENESS
How do they avoid publishing a policy-compliant but ugly/weak main image?
Do they explicitly score for attractiveness, clarity, product occupancy, trustworthiness, catalog quality, clickability, or marketplace conversion fitness?

6. FALLBACK STRATEGIES
What happens when none of the available images are fully compliant?
What is the best-practice fallback:
- automated remediation
- alternate source fetch
- human review
- item rejection
- marketplace-specific downgrade path

7. SYSTEM DESIGN
What is the common architecture pattern used by the best software?
Is it usually:
- candidate ranking + rule engine
- marketplace-specific policy templates
- AI remediation layer
- confidence threshold + manual review
- feedback loop from marketplace moderation results

8. LEARNING LOOP
Do top systems appear to learn from:
- marketplace rejections
- moderation flags
- conversion performance
- image CTR
- listing suppression events

9. RECOMMENDATION FOR IVAN RESELLER
Based on all findings, what is the strongest recommended image pipeline architecture for Ivan Reseller specifically?

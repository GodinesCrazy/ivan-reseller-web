## P6 First Product Strategy Reframe

### Objective
Give the current supplier stack one last fair eBay US chance without lowering any threshold.

### Strategy Change
P6 moved away from the repeatedly weak query families and switched to a narrower commodity-first set:

- `adhesive cable clips`
- `silicone cable ties`
- `webcam cover`
- `screen cleaning cloth`
- `adhesive wall hook`

### Why These Queries
They are:

- small and light
- cheaper on average
- less policy-heavy
- more likely to ship broadly to the US
- better suited to a first low-risk validated product

### Additional Safe Improvements
- stronger blocked-term filtering for decorative/branded/noisy results
- better candidate ranking before expensive validation
- lower preferred source-price bands for first-product economics
- clearer separation of commercial-fit failures from supplier-stock failures

### What P6 Explicitly Avoided
- relaxing stock or shipping checks
- relaxing fee or margin rules
- trying heavier or more complex categories
- repeating the exact same weak query mix from earlier phases

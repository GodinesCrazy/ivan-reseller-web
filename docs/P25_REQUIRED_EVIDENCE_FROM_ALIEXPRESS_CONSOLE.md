# P25 Required Evidence From AliExpress Console

## Mandatory Evidence To Bring Back
- Screenshot or text of the exact dropshipping app name
- Screenshot or text of the exact app status
- Screenshot or text of the exact AppKey for the dropshipping app
- Confirmation whether backend AppKey prefix `522578...` matches the console AppKey
- Screenshot or text of the API/method permissions for the dropshipping app
- Explicit confirmation whether `aliexpress.logistics.buyer.freight.calculate` appears in the approved or available API list
- Explicit confirmation whether freight requires separate provisioning, approval, or another app family
- Evidence of any reauthorization performed on the dropshipping app
- Evidence of whether new session tokens were issued after reauth
- Any platform error message shown after reauth or after checking permissions
- Any indication that the current app is in the wrong state, wrong family, or wrong product line

## Preferred Formatting For Return
- 1 screenshot or text snippet per checklist step
- 1 short note saying `good` or `bad` for each step
- 1 final note saying either:
  - `freight should now be callable`
  - `freight still externally blocked`
